About the College

Government Polytechnic Ausari Khurud provides high-quality technical education in a serene and skill-driven atmosphere. With a strong focus on practical learning, the college is a top choice in rural Maharashtra.

Courses Offered

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Computer Engineering

Campus & Facilities

Set in a calm rural backdrop, the campus includes state-of-the-art labs, smart classrooms, a library, workshop areas, and an eco-friendly campus with green initiatives.

Faculty

Faculty members are well-qualified and dedicated to hands-on education and student mentoring. Regular training programs keep their skills industry-aligned.

Hostel

Separate hostels for boys and girls with good food, peaceful surroundings, and basic amenities like Wi-Fi and common rooms.

Placement Records

With growing ties to local industries and training partners, the college has steadily improved its placement performance, especially in government and private sector companies.

Government Polytechnic Ausari Khurud

Leading Technical Education in Ausari Khurud

About the College

Government Polytechnic Ausari Khurud was established in 2008 to cater to the technical education needs of rural Maharashtra. The college is known for its serene campus and focus on practical learning. It is affiliated with MSBTE and approved by AICTE.

The college offers modern facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, workshops, and a library. It regularly organizes industry visits and skill development programs to ensure students are prepared for professional challenges.

History

Since its establishment in 2008, Government Polytechnic Ausari Khurud has been dedicated to providing quality technical education. The college started with a few diploma courses and has expanded its offerings over the years. It has played a significant role in empowering students from rural areas.

The institution has consistently upgraded its infrastructure and curriculum to meet industry standards. Its alumni network is strong, with graduates excelling in various fields.

Courses Offered

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Computer Engineering

Campus & Facilities

Set in a calm rural backdrop, the campus includes state-of-the-art labs, smart classrooms, a library, workshop areas, and an eco-friendly campus with green initiatives.

Faculty

Faculty members are well-qualified and dedicated to hands-on education and student mentoring. Regular training programs keep their skills industry-aligned.

Hostel

Separate hostels for boys and girls with good food, peaceful surroundings, and basic amenities like Wi-Fi and common rooms.

Placement Records

With growing ties to local industries and training partners, the college has steadily improved its placement performance, especially in government and private sector companies.

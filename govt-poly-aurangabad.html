Government Polytechnic Aurangabad

Leading Technical Education in Aurangabad

About the College

Government Polytechnic Aurangabad was established in 1955 and is one of the oldest polytechnic colleges in Maharashtra. The college is renowned for its academic excellence and state-of-the-art facilities. It is affiliated with MSBTE and approved by AICTE.

The institution offers a wide range of diploma courses and has a strong focus on research and innovation. It regularly collaborates with industries to provide students with practical exposure.

History

Established in 1955, Government Polytechnic Aurangabad has a rich legacy of producing skilled engineers and technicians. The college has been a pioneer in technical education and has contributed significantly to the development of the region.

Over the years, the college has expanded its infrastructure and introduced new courses to keep up with the evolving needs of the industry. Its alumni are well-placed in reputed organizations worldwide.

Courses Offered

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Computer Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electronics & Telecommunication

Campus & Facilities

The campus includes modern laboratories, Wi-Fi enabled classrooms, dedicated workshops, and a central library with thousands of volumes. Students enjoy facilities such as a cafeteria, indoor games, and tech clubs.

Faculty

The faculty comprises experienced professors, many with industrial exposure and research backgrounds. Regular workshops and training programs keep them updated with the latest trends in engineering education.

Hostel

Separate hostel facilities are available for boys and girls. Rooms are clean, well-ventilated, and include internet access. The hostel mess serves hygienic and nutritious meals.

Placement Records

The placement cell actively connects students with top recruiters. Alumni have secured jobs in reputed companies and public sector units across India.
